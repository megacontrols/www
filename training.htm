Agency Approval
UL (Underwriters laboratories, Inc.), ULC (Underwriters' Laboratories of Canada), CSA (Canadian Standards Association) and FM (Factory Mutual Research) are independent, third-party testing laboratories whose primary mission is to test products for safety, and in some cases performance. These organizations use industry recognized standards to judge a product, and grant "approval" based on those standards.
