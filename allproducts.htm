BACnet MS/TP EQUIPMENT
 
 

BACnet is a Data Communication Protocol for Building Automation and Control Networks. Developed under the auspices of the American Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air-Conditioning Engineers (ASHRAE), BACnet is an American national standard, a European standard, a national standard in more than 30 countries, and an ISO global standard. The protocol is supported and maintained by ASHRAE Standing Standard Project Committee 135 whose members have created and provided the content for the BACnet Website.

 

BACnet MS/TP  and TCP/IP systems in relation to automated networks for commercial applications are systems where products from multiple vendors conform to a BACnet protocol enabling full interoperability across a unified network.

 

Mega Controls provides Building Owners/Facilities Managers with BACnet field  devices to allow seamless integration into their Building Automation Systems.

These high-quality actuator-driven ball valves combine the performance of globe valves with the economy of ball valves - providing the best of both worlds. The VFB30 Series automatic flow balancing control ball valves are equipped with a propeller-type flow measuring device to provide a flow control signal to the controller integrated in the ball valve actuator. The ball valve will be positioned to its optimum operating position according to the control signal from the field controller until the preset flow limit is reached and then the preset flow rate limit will be maintained. The VFB30 Series ball valves are equipped with a characterized constricted channel at the valve inlet in which choked flow is used to control the flow rate of water. This characterized constricted channel design provides very high rangeability and excellent equal percentage flow characteristics.

 The VFB30 Series automatic flow balancing control ball valves are equipped with non-spring return electric rotary-motion actuators of modulating control actions. Standard power supply is 24V 50/60Hz and all actuators come with a manual override lever for manually positioning the valve when the actuator is not powered.

 The VFB30 Series automatic flow balancing control ball valves assure that all terminal equipment will perform as specified and HVAC system will operate under accurately and dynamically balanced conditions.
